>The court found that the plaintiffs did "not explain how the rights allegedly violated by the [public health order] are fundamental." 2021 WL 4145746, at *5. "[I]ndeed nowhere," said the court, did the plaintiffs "address how the right to work in a hospital or attend the State Fair, unvaccinated and during a pandemic, is 'deeply rooted in this Nations history and tradition.'" Id.

Source: [Federal Court: Anti-Vaxxers Do Not Have a Constitutional or Statutory Right to Endanger Everyone Else - Drug & Device Law](https://www.druganddevicelawblog.com/2021/09/federal-court-anti-vaxxers-do-not-have-a-constitutional-or-statutory-right-to-endanger-everyone-else.html)

This has been the case for over a hundred years so it's no surprise but I'm glad this was the decision.
